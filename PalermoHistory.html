The city is a blend of cultures, arts, and traditions. The capital of Sicily is undoubtedly one of the most visited cities on the island.

It is the perfect vacation destination, as it combines history, art, nature, relaxation, and entertainment. Palermo stands out thanks to its authentic atmosphere and diversity.
